About Blue Leaf Bookstore

About Blue Leaf


We are open Monday through Friday from 10 am to 9 pm, and Saturday and Sunday from 10 am to 7 pm. We close only on Thanksgiving, Christmas, and New Year’s Day.

Our books pertain to computer services and design. We have a heavy supply in SQL, Databases, C++, and many other computer languages. If you are a student majoring in Computer Science or are interested in taking computer classes, this bookstore is a must!

Our Staff

There are five of us, all dedicated booksellers, and all of us active and avid readers, with a wide range of interests. All of us have worked at other bookstores before coming to Blue Leaf, and we put this experience to use with knowledgeable, personable, and friendly service. You’ll find our hand-written book recommendations in every section of the store, and we are happy to discuss your options for the next book to read or give as a gift.

Our Services

Gift-wrapping is always free, and we have an assortment of papers to choose from. If we are out of a title or don’t carry it, we are happy to special order it at no extra charge. Most special orders arrive in a week or less.
